Mariyam Global Traders is a Tamil Nadu-based export company committed to delivering premium-quality natural products with honesty, consistency, and global standards.

Rooted in the rich agricultural heritage of South India, we specialize in Moringa Products, Curry Leaves and Agro-Based Products carefully sourced and processed to maintain freshness, purity and nutritional value.

Our focus is not only on supplying products but on building long-term relationships through transparent communication, reliable service and professional export practices.

As we continue to grow, our vision is to become a trusted global partner in the natural and agro-based export industry.
